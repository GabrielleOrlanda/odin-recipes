Choose a Filipino Classic Recipe

    Chicken Adobo

      Stuff your face with one of the Filipino classics, the Chicken Adobo. A dish that well represents the Philippines.

      As many have their own recipe for Chicken Adobo, this recipe is one of the recipes that you can learn and cook and maybe learn your own version of the classic dish!

    Fish Stew with Miso (Sinigang sa Miso)

      Another classic Filipino dish of a soup that is often eaten with rice as a side.

      Many variations exist for the Sinigang recipe to try and its very delicious soup is something to come back to.

    Chicken Tinola

      A simple yet delicious Filpino classic dish of a soup full of chicken and Chayote, the Chicken Tinola.

      It's flavorful broth is something to be loved alongside rice.
